The first release of Onym.

- End-to-end encrypted group chats
- No phone number or email — your identity is a key you own
- Create a group and share a single invite link
- Bring your own Nostr relays and Blossom media servers
- Multiple identities, switchable with a tap
- Recovery-phrase backup

Thanks for trying Onym. Feedback and issues: github.com/onymchat/onym-ios
