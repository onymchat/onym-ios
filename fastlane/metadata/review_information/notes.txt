Onym needs no account, login, or demo credentials.

On first launch the app auto-creates a local cryptographic identity — there is no sign-up, phone number, or email. Everything works from a fresh install.

To exercise the core flow:
1. Open the app — an identity is created automatically (Settings tab shows it with an invite QR).
2. On the Chats tab, tap the compose button to create an encrypted group, then tap "Share invite link" to get a join link.
3. A second install (or another device) can open that link to request to join; the group admin approves it in "Join requests".

Notes for review:
- Messages are end-to-end encrypted; Onym operates no central message server. By default the app talks to public Nostr relays (message/invite transport) and Blossom servers (media) on Stellar testnet — no real funds are involved.
- Face ID / biometrics is used only to gate viewing the local recovery phrase; it never leaves the device.
- No user tracking, no ads, no third-party analytics.
